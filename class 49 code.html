Interesting facts about camels

  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for throny plants other animals can't eat
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms
  • Mother camels carry their calves up to 14 months before giving birth

Interesting facts about chameleons

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their head
  • Chameleons have a extremely powerfull tongues
  • Chameleons like it hot
  • Chameleons take a while to hatch but don't live for very long

Interesting facts about pigeons

  • Pigeons understand space and time
  • Pigeons can find their way back to their nest from 1300 miles away
  • Pigeons are very good at multi-tasking
  • If pigeons are given the right conditions , they're as good at identifying cancer as doctors
